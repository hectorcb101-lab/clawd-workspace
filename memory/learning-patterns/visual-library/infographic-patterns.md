# Infographic Patterns & Cheat Sheets

**Quick-reference visual summaries for learned concepts**

---

## What Are Infographics (In This Context)?

**Not:** Decorative graphics with icons and colors  
**But:** Dense, structured quick-reference sheets

**Purpose:**
- Post-learning summary
- Quick refresher before retention checks
- Exam prep condensed notes
- At-a-glance concept review

**Philosophy:** Maximum information density, minimum clutter

---
