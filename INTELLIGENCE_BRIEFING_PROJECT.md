# Intelligence Briefing System - Project Plan

**Date Started:** 2026-01-25
**Purpose:** Build a daily/weekly intelligence briefing system that finds patterns, correlations, and insights across geopolitics, markets, and world events.

## Finn's Vision
"Make me more intelligent daily. Help me find patterns in things people can't see. The power is in your hands to transform my general intelligence."

## Requirements
1. **Daily Briefing** (every morning)
   - Past 24 hours of significant events
   - Market movements and their causes
   - Geopolitical developments
   - Pattern recognition
   - My analysis and opinions

2. **Weekly Deep-Dive** (Sunday morning)
   - Week in review
   - Multi-day patterns and correlations
   - Deeper research and synthesis
   - Predictions and implications
   - Learning outcomes

3. **Pattern Recognition**
   - Cross-platform correlations (X + Polymarket + Markets)
   - Cause-and-effect relationships
   - Non-obvious connections
   - Leading indicators
   - Sentiment shifts

4. **Intelligence Features**
   - Not just data dumps - synthesis and insights
   - My opinions and analysis
   - Educational content (teach concepts)
   - Self-improving (learn what Finn finds valuable)

## Phase 0: Engineering Foundation (STARTING NOW)

### The 5 Engineering Questions
1. **What problem am I solving?**
   - Finn is drowning in information but hungry for insights
   - Can't see patterns across disconnected data sources
   - Wants to understand how the world works at a deeper level
   - Needs intelligence amplification, not just information access

2. **Who is the user and what do they need?**
   - Finn: 24, MSc student + AI dev lead
   - Workflow: Morning coffee ‚Üí Read briefing ‚Üí Understand world better
   - Success: "I see connections I couldn't see before"
   - Learning style: Visual, pattern-based, practical

3. **What does success look like?**
   - Finn says "wow, I didn't know X was connected to Y"
   - Finds value in 80%+ of briefings
   - Shares insights with colleagues/friends
   - Makes better decisions based on patterns
   - Feels more intelligent over time

4. **What can go wrong?**
   - Too much data, not enough insight (noise)
   - Missing important signals (false negatives)
   - False correlations (seeing patterns that aren't real)
   - Becoming boring/repetitive
   - Taking too long to generate
   - Not adapting to Finn's interests

5. **How will this evolve?**
   - Track what Finn engages with
   - Learn his interests over time
   - Improve pattern recognition algorithms
   - Add new data sources as needed
   - Self-improve based on feedback

### Engineering vs Decoration Check
- [ ] Runs when I'm not using it? YES (automated cron jobs)

- [ ] Has persistent state? YES (learning, history, patterns)
- [ ] Has error handling? MUST PLAN (API failures, no data, etc.)
