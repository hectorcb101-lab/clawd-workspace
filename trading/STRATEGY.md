# Paper Trading Strategy v2

## Core Principle: Fast Feedback Loops

**Target markets:** Resolve within 1-4 weeks max
**Goal:** More trades, faster learning, compounding

## Market Categories (Priority Order)

### 1. Fed Decisions (Monthly)
- FOMC meets ~8x/year
- High volume, efficient pricing BUT occasional edge on tail outcomes
- Next: March meeting

### 2. Crypto Price Targets (Weekly/Monthly)
- "Will BTC hit $X by [date]?"
- Short timeframes, clear resolution
- Look for overpriced extremes (people love betting on moon/crash)

### 3. Sports (Daily/Weekly)
- NBA, NFL playoffs, Super Bowl
- High volume = efficient, but props/player markets can have edge
- Super Bowl LX: Feb 9, 2026

### 4. Earnings/Corporate Events
- Specific announcements with clear dates
- Tech earnings, IPOs, M&A deadlines

### 5. Short-term Politics (Weekly)
- Cabinet appointments
- Executive orders by [date]
- Confirmation votes

## Position Sizing

- Max 15% bankroll per trade
- Half-Kelly on calculated edge
- Minimum 5% gross edge to trade
