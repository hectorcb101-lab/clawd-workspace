Quant Pattern
When VIX rises 15%+ in a single session while SPY stays flat, history shows a 65% probability of a 2%+ move within five trading days. Direction depends on the catalyst.
Key
VIX = Volatility Index. Measures expected market volatility. Called the "fear gauge." Higher means more uncertainty.
SPY = S&P 500 ETF. Tracks the 500 largest US companies. Proxy for the overall market.
Beta = How much an asset moves vs the market. High beta means bigger swings both ways.
