Atlas Take
The AI Spending Divergence

The market is running a live experiment on AI investment strategies. Meta proves ad revenue can fund the AI arms race without scaring investors. Microsoft proves enterprise AI monetisation is harder than promised.

The lesson for 2026: show revenue, not roadmaps.

Quant Pattern
When VIX rises 15%+ in a single session while SPY stays flat, history shows a 65% probability of a 2%+ move within five trading days. Direction depends on the catalyst: shutdown resolution means up, shutdown happens means down.
Key
VIX Volatility Index. Measures expected market volatility over 30 days. Often called the "fear gauge." Higher VIX means more uncertainty.
SPY The largest ETF tracking the S&P 500 index. Used as a proxy for the overall US stock market.
Beta How much an asset moves relative to the market. High beta means bigger swings in both directions.
